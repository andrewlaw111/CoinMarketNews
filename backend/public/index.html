About

CoinMarketNews news is an iOS and Android App developed by Andrew Law and Didier Krux.

With a beautiful and easy interface, CoinMarketNews features prices, news, and info on over 1,500 coins. Whether you are an experienced crypto trader or have newly discovered this exciting technology, CoinMarketNews is where you can get the crypto info you need.

Features include:

  • Price information on over 1,500 coins
  • Favorites tracking
  • Coin price sorting
  • Latest news from various sources
  • Custom news and price alerts per coin
  • Coin information on many coins
  • Dark mode
